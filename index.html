Welcome to the
Red Cross Youth Club

Red Cross Youth (RCY) functions as the youth arm of the Red Cross, aiming to educate and empower young people to serve their communities through humanitarian activities. Its roles include members, officers, advisers, and volunteers who work together to promote Red Cross principles, lead youth initiatives, and participate in disaster response, health education, and community service.

What is Red Cross

RED CROSS YOUTH LOGO

Red Cross Youth logo has a big red cross in the middle. That red cross is a worldwide symbol of helping others. It shows that we are part of a global group that cares for people, especially during emergencies, disasters, or health problems.

Around the red cross, you’ll see a circle that says “Red Cross Youth” and “Philippine Red Cross.” This simply means that we are the youth branch of the Philippine Red Cross. We’re not just random volunteers. We belong to a recognized and trusted organization in the whole country.

The colors also have meaning. Red stands for action and bravery, like we’re ready to help anytime. White means kindness and clean intentions because we serve without judging anyone. And the Blue color represents trust and unity, just like our Philippine identity.

The shape of the cross, with equal sides, shows equality. It reminds us that everyone deserves help no matter who they are or where they come from. That’s what makes the logo so powerful and known everywhere.
