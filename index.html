What is Red Cross

The RCY Logo

Red Cross Youth logo has a big red cross in the middle. That red cross is a worldwide symbol of helping others. It shows that we are part of a global group that cares for people, especially during emergencies, disasters, or health problems.

Around the red cross, you'll see a circle that says "Red Cross Youth" and "Philippine Red Cross." This simply means that we are the youth branch of the Philippine Red Cross. We belong to a recognized and trusted organization in the whole country.

The colors also have meaning. Red stands for action and bravery. White means kindness and clean intentions. And Blue represents trust and unity.

The shape of the cross, with equal sides, shows equality — everyone deserves help no matter who they are or where they come from.
